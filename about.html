What is GEAR Club?


Hello! We are the Gavilan Engineering and Research Club, or GEAR Club for short. Our objective for this club is for it to be a welcoming space for people from any kind of background to come together and collaborate on projects they are passionate about. We are open for any Gavilan student to join, even if your major is not Engineering or STEM related. We have meetings every Thursday from 12:50pm-1:50pm on Zoom.


We are currently working on the Smart Sustainable Garden Project! Our overall objective for this project is to inspire others to take action and create their own smart sustainable garden! As we progress with this project , the online materials and a step by step process of how we are creating our garden will be posted on this site. We will also be posting overall progress and little blogs relsted to running a club. To learn more about what it is and maybe how you can help click this link or navigate to our Projects page!
