Hello! We are the Gavilan Engineering and Research Club, or GEAR Club for short. Our objective for this club is for it to be a welcoming space for people of all kinds of background to come together and collaborate on projects they are passionate about. We are open for any Gavilan student to join, even if your major is not Engineering or STEM related. We have meetings every Thursday from 12:50pm-1:50pm on Zoom.
